Josh's Top Movie Recommendations

My favorite 3 movies to enjoy.

Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse

Everything about this movie is perfection: the animation, the soundtrack, the story and the action. It's the closest thing to a flawless movie.

Baby Driver

Again, amazing soundtrack. And, the filmography by Edgar Wright is genius.

Logan

Gotta have a superhero movie, and this one is a premiere adaptation of an all-time favorite character.
